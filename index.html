Welcome

Psychology helps children understand their thoughts, feelings, behaviour, friendships and healthy habits. Learning about psychology helps children become happier, kinder and more confident.

The Human Brain

The brain is the control centre of our body. It helps us think, learn, remember, solve problems, control our emotions and make good decisions. A healthy brain grows stronger with sleep, healthy food, exercise and learning new things.

Understanding Emotions

  • 😊 Happy
  • 😢 Sad
  • 😡 Angry
  • 😨 Scared
  • 😍 Loved
  • 😌 Calm

Every emotion is normal. Talking to parents, teachers or friends can help.

Memory Tips

  • 📚 Read every day
  • 😴 Sleep 8–10 hours
  • 🥗 Eat healthy food
  • 🏃 Exercise daily
  • 🧩 Play memory games

Healthy Habits

  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Play outside every day.
  • Be kind to others.
  • Keep a positive attitude.
  • Wash your hands regularly.

Managing Stress

  • Take deep breaths.
  • Talk to your parents.
  • Listen to music.
  • Draw or colour.
  • Play with friends.

Fun Psychology Facts

  • Your brain keeps learning every day.
  • Smiling can improve your mood.
  • Sleep helps your brain remember things.
  • Exercise makes your brain healthier.
  • Helping others makes you feel happier.
